## Pedagogical Structure

### Content Design
- Each module is a self-contained lesson from the *Oral Health Bulletin*
- Written **for clinicians**, not just researchers — practical language with scientific grounding
- All claims tagged to [[02_Evidence_Tiers]] framework
- Modules build on each other within a series but can be read independently

### Series Progression Logic

```
Series 1: Metabolic context (diabetes as exemplar of bidirectional oral-systemic link)
    │
    ▼
Series 2: Mechanistic effector (OMVs as the "how" of oral → systemic transmission)
    │
    ▼
Series 3: Systems framework (information theory as lens for understanding dysbiosis)
    │
    ▼
Series 4: Ecological foundation (biofilm as the upstream biological system)
```

### Learning Arc
1. **Why it matters:** Diabetes shows oral-systemic bidirectionality is real and clinically relevant
2. **What's doing it:** OMVs carry virulence cargo from mouth to body
3. **How it propagates:** Information collapse explains how local dysbiosis becomes systemic dysfunction
4. **Where it starts:** Biofilm ecology determines whether the system stays healthy or tips into disease
